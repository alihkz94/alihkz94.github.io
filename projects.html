EUKARYOME

A comprehensive rRNA gene reference database for the identification of all eukaryotic taxa. Enables high-precision environmental DNA classification across microbiome and ecological studies.

Statistical Method

DEASS

A novel statistical framework for differential expression analysis in RNA-seq data, designed to improve robustness and reduce false discovery rates in multi-group comparisons with complex experimental designs.

Algorithm Evaluation

Long-read Chimera Detection

Systematic benchmarking of chimera detection algorithms for PacBio and Oxford Nanopore long-read amplicon data, quantifying error rates, sensitivity, and specificity across diverse marker regions and community complexities.

Single-Cell Genomics

Integrative single-cell analysis of cancer samples using 10X Chromium scRNA-seq and CROP-seq libraries. Applied ML models (scVI, TensorFlow, PyTorch) for clonal evolution, CNV detection, and gene set enrichment.
