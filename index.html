01About

From clinical single-cell data
to environmental genomics.

I am a bioinformatician at the University of Tartu, where my PhD focuses on the analysis of long DNA markers for microbiome and fungal community studies. My core work covers amplicon-sequencing and metagenomics pipeline development, long-read quality control, and benchmarking of chimera-detection algorithms.

Before my PhD I worked in clinical genomics — single-cell and bulk transcriptomics, WGS/WES variant calling, and CRISPR-screen analysis for cancer research. I contribute to the PipeCraft2 pipeline platform and the EUKARYOME rRNA reference database, and I am part of the TREC (Traversing European Coastlines) sampling initiative.

I am open to industry roles and collaborations in multi-omics data analysis, bioinformatics pipeline engineering, and applied computational biology.

02Research

Six core competencies across the multi-omics stack.

From clinical cancer genomics to continental-scale biodiversity, built on reproducible, benchmarked methods.

Amplicon sequencing & metagenomics

Profiling microbial and eukaryotic communities from environmental DNA using amplicon (barcoding and metabarcoding) and shotgun metagenomic sequencing — built on the containerized, Nextflow-based PipeCraft2 with EUKARYOME as the taxonomic backbone.

Long-read sequencing & QC

Quality-control and denoising workflows for PacBio and Oxford Nanopore long reads — benchmarking filtering strategies and their downstream effect on biological interpretation.

Algorithm benchmarking & validation

Comparative evaluation of chimera-detection algorithms for long-read amplicons — resolving which approaches minimize false positives while preserving true biological signal. Published in PeerJ (2025).

Single-cell & bulk transcriptomics

scRNA-seq, bulk RNA-seq and CRISPR (CROP-seq) screen analysis for cancer research — cell-type annotation, trajectory and clonal-evolution inference, and CNV detection, using ML frameworks such as scVI, TensorFlow and PyTorch.

Workflow & pipeline engineering

Reproducible, containerized workflows in Nextflow and Snakemake (Docker, Singularity), deployable on HPC clusters (SLURM) and cloud (AWS) — from one-off analyses to production tools used by the research community.

Variant calling — WGS/WES

Germline and somatic variant calling, structural-variant and CNV detection across short- and long-read platforms — including GPU-accelerated pipelines (Nvidia Parabricks) for clinical and fungal genomics.

Tools, databases & studies.

PipeCraft2

pipecraft2 organization · University of Tartu

A modular, containerized pipeline for amplicon sequencing and metabarcoding — end-to-end from raw reads to community tables, deployable on HPC and cloud. I develop new modules and maintain existing workflows.

EUKARYOME

University of Tartu · Tedersoo Lab

A comprehensive rRNA gene reference database for identifying all eukaryotic taxa, powering environmental DNA classification. Published in Database (Oxford), 2024.

  • Phylogenetics
  • Curation

SnakeEUK

University of Tartu

A Snakemake pipeline that reformats eukaryotic rRNA reference sequences into tool-specific formats — DADA2, QIIME2, SINTAX, Mothur — handling encoding, taxonomy normalization and placeholder cleaning for downstream classifiers.

  • Snakemake
  • Biopython
  • SeqKit

DEASS

University of Tartu

A statistical method for differential expression in RNA-seq — designed to improve power and reduce false discovery in multi-group comparisons with complex experimental designs.

  • R
  • RNA-seq
  • Method dev

Long-read chimera benchmarking

University of Tartu · PeerJ 2025

Systematic evaluation of chimera-detection algorithms for PacBio and Nanopore amplicons — revealing high false-positive rates and the genuine sequences discarded during quality filtering.

  • Python
  • R
  • PacBio
  • Nanopore

TREC — European Coastlines

EMBL · International consortium

An international biodiversity sampling initiative spanning European coastlines. I analyze amplicon-sequencing and metagenomics datasets generated across multiple countries and sequencing platforms.
