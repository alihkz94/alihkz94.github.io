About

Decoding Life
Through Data

I am a Bioinformatician at the University of Tartu, specialized in long DNA marker analysis, microbiome and fungal eDNA barcoding, and scalable omics pipelines.

My work combines molecular and computational methods to design robust analysis workflows and high-quality reference databases for environmental and biomedical datasets — bridging ecology, genomics, and machine learning.

Currently contributing to PipeCraft2, EUKARYOME, and the TREC international coastal sampling initiative across European coastlines.
