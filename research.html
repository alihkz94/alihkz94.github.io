Research Focus

Current work spans environmental genomics, long-read analysis, and computational methods for scalable and reproducible bioinformatics.

Metabarcoding & Metagenomics

Bioinformatics pipelines for microbiome and fungal eDNA analysis, including PipeCraft2 and EUKARYOME workflows.

Long-Read Amplicon Analysis

Evaluation of chimera-detection methods for PacBio and Oxford Nanopore long-read amplicon datasets.

Single-Cell Analysis

scRNA-seq and CROP-seq analysis for CRISPR screens with large-scale donor-level studies and ML-driven interpretation.

Transcriptomics

Differential expression and robust sample-wise statistical frameworks, including DEASS method development.

Environmental Genomics

International sampling and computational analysis in the TREC project across European coastlines.

Pipeline Development

Reproducible workflows with Nextflow, Snakemake, and containerized deployments.
