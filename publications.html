Invisible passengers: the diversity and invasion risks of fungi and bacteria transported via footwear of international airport passengers

Biological Invasions (Springer), 2026

Niloufar Hagh-Doust, Ali Hakimzadeh, John Yangyuoru Kupagme, Glen Dierickx, Ovidiu Copot, Leho Tedersoo

This study evaluates microbial transport risks through passenger footwear and highlights the hidden mobility of fungal and bacterial communities across international hubs. Findings emphasize biosecurity relevance and the need for surveillance strategies in high-throughput travel pathways.

Published Article

Benchmarking chimera detection algorithms for long-read amplicons

PeerJ, 2025

Ali Hakimzadeh, Vladimir Mikryukov, Leho Tedersoo, Sten Anslan

This paper provides a comparative benchmark of chimera-detection tools in long-read amplicon workflows and highlights tool-specific behavior under realistic sequencing conditions. It offers practical guidance for method selection and parameter tuning in PacBio and Oxford Nanopore pipelines.
