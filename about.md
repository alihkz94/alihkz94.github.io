I am a bioinformatician at the University of Tartu (Institute of Ecology and Earth Sciences), working on the analysis of long DNA markers for microbiome and fungal eDNA studies. My research focuses on metabarcoding and metagenomics pipeline development, long-read amplicon quality control, and chimera detection algorithm benchmarking.

I also work on single-cell and bulk transcriptomics, WGS/WES variant calling, and CRISPR screen analysis. I contribute to the [PipeCraft2](https://github.com/pipecraft2/pipecraft) pipeline platform and the [EUKARYOME](https://eukaryome.org/) rRNA reference database, and I have participated in the [TREC](https://www.embl.org/about/info/trec/) (Traversing European Coastlines) international sampling initiative.

I am open to scientific collaboration in computational biology, environmental genomics, and long-read sequencing analysis.
